Why Rusty Links?

No tabs. No chaos. No nonsense.

Self-hosted bookmark management that just works.

Auto Metadata

Titles, descriptions, and logos extracted automatically on save.

Full-Text Search

Find any bookmark instantly with advanced filtering by tag, language, license, and category.

Secure by Design

JWT authentication with Argon2id hashing and refresh tokens keep your bookmarks private.

GitHub Integration

Stars, languages, and licenses auto-detected for any GitHub repo.

Hierarchical Categories

Up to 3 levels of categories to organize your links your way.

Docker Ready

One command deployment. Full control over your data and infrastructure.
